Common Questions

📍 Which states do you deliver to? +
We deliver to 5 states: Telangana, Andhra Pradesh, Goa, Maharashtra, and Karnataka. We pick up from your door and deliver to the door at the destination. If you are not sure we cover your area, just call us on 9322099980 — we will tell you in 2 minutes!
🚛 What kind of goods can I send? +
You can send parcels and packages (small or large boxes), industrial goods (machine parts, steel, raw materials), household items (furniture, TV, appliances when shifting home), and business or shop stock (wholesale goods, retail items). Not sure? Just call and ask — we'll help you!
📞 How do I book? Is it complicated? +
It is very simple! Just call us on 93220 99980 or 97699 01140, or send a WhatsApp message on 93220 99980. Tell us: (1) where to pick up, (2) where to deliver, and (3) what you are sending. That's it — we do the rest!
🏠 Do you come to my house to pick up? +
Yes, 100%! Our team comes directly to your home, shop, factory, or warehouse to pick up your goods. You do NOT need to go to any depot or office. And we also deliver directly to the receiver's door — no extra trips needed!
💰 How much does it cost? +
The price depends on: how heavy or big the item is, and how far it is going. We give you the full price before we start — no hidden charges or surprises later. Just call us on 93220 99980 for an instant quote — it takes only 2-3 minutes!
⏰ When can I call you? +
You can call or WhatsApp us any time. Our support is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Whether it's early morning or late night — we are there to help you!
