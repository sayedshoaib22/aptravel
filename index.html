Which states do you deliver to? +
We currently serve Telangana, Andhra Pradesh, Goa, Maharashtra, and Karnataka with complete door-to-door pickup and delivery. Not sure if we reach your exact area? Call us on 9322099980 and we'll confirm in minutes.
What types of cargo do you accept? +
We handle four categories: Parcels & Packages (personal and commercial), Industrial Material (machinery, raw materials, bulk loads), Household & Relocation (furniture, appliances, full moves), and Commercial & Business Goods (wholesale, retail, FMCG, B2B). How do I book a shipment? +
Simply call us on +91 93220 99980 or +91 97699 01140, or send us a WhatsApp message on 93220 99980. Tell us your pickup location, destination, and cargo type — we'll confirm the booking and schedule a pickup time that works for you.
Do you offer door-to-door pickup and delivery? +
Yes — 100% door-to-door is our core service. Our team picks up from your home, office, factory, or warehouse and delivers directly to the recipient's address. There is no need to drop off at a depot or collection point.
How much does cargo delivery cost? +
Pricing depends on the weight, size, type of cargo, and the distance between pickup and delivery locations. We offer competitive, transparent pricing with no hidden charges. Call us on 93220 99980 for an instant quote — it takes just a few minutes.
